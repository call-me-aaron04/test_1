[05 / 11]Neutron & Hybrid

Where lead alone isn't enough.

Beyond ~10 MeV photons and in cyclotron environments, secondary neutrons dominate the radiation field. ALMATIN engineers layered composites — borated polyethylene, HDPE and high-density concrete — to attenuate the fast and thermal neutron spectrum together with their capture gammas.

  • N · 01
    Borated Polyethylene (5% B) Thermal neutron capture via ¹⁰B(n,α) — 150–250mm typical for proton therapy mazes.
  • N · 02
    Maze Geometry & Direct Door Design Multi-leg maze attenuation analysis, NCRP-151 Kersey method, leakage-streaming corrections.
  • N · 03
    Activation & Decay Modeling Concrete and air activation prediction · waste corridor design · post-shutdown survey plans.
  • N · 04
    Hot Lab & Isotope Flow Dose calibrator enclosures, automated dispensing shields, F-18/Ga-68 transport pathways.
